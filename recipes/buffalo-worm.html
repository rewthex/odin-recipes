Buffalo Worm Cauliflower Wings

Thanks to the batter with lots of flavoursome spices and the mouth-watering sauce, these cauliflower florets taste like BBQ wings but with no chickens involved. And best of all, you can cook them up in only 30 minutes – bonkers!

INGREDIENTS

  • Panko Breadcrumbs – 150g
  • Cornflour – 100g
  • Cauliflower – 1
  • Coriander – small handful
  • Sesame Seeds – 2 tbsp
  • Spring Onions – 3
  • Red Chilli – 1
  • Sunflower Oil – 200ml
Batter:
  • Buffalo Worm Powder – 30g
  • Cornflour – 50g
  • Plain Flour – 30g
  • Cold Soda Water – 150ml
  • Coconut Milk – 50ml
  • Paprika – 2 tsp
  • Cayenne Pepper – 2 tsp
  • Garlic Powder – 2 tsp
  • Onion Powder – 2 tsp
  • Salt – 1 tsp
Sauce:
  • Oyster Sauce – 3 tbsp
  • Honey – 4 tbsp
  • Ketchup – 1 ½ tbsp
  • Sesame Oil – 2 tsp
  • Soy Sauce – 3 tbsp
  • Lime Juice – 1
  • Siracha – 100 ml
  • Sweet Chilli Sauce – 3 tbsp

METHOD

PREPARE THE CAULIFLOWER | 15 mins
  1. Break the cauliflower head into smaller florets.
  2. Blanch them in boiling water for 3 secs and let them steam dry.
  3. Once they are dry, roll them in cornflour.
  4. Combine all the batter ingredients, do not mix too much.
  5. Make the cauliflower wings by dunking each floret into the batter and then into the breadcrumbs.
  6. Heat a deep pan with sunflower oil in it on a medium-high heat (you want it to be about 180°C)
  7. Put each floret into the oil until they are all golden brown in colour.
  8. Rest on a tea towel or drying rack to absorb some of the oil.
MAKE THE SAUCE | 10 mins
  1. Weight out all the ingredients for the sauce.
  2. Heat the mixture up in a saucepan on a low-medium heat until it thickens.
SERVE | 5 mins
  1. Finely chop the coriander.
  2. Thinly slice the red chilli and spring onions.
  3. Heat the sauce with the cauliflower wings in it.
  4. Garnish with the coriander, spring onions, chillies, and sesame seeds.
